Courses We Offer

Manila University offers a wide range of programs under the College of Computer Studies (CCS), the College of Engineering, and other academic disciplines, preparing students for technology-driven and industry-ready careers.

Computer Studies Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

Focuses on computing theories, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and advanced programming concepts.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Hands-on training in networking, cybersecurity, database management, and web development for IT industry careers.

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS)

Integrates business and IT, developing skills in systems analysis, enterprise solutions, and IT project management.

Bachelor of Science in Entertainment & Multimedia Computing (BSEMC)

Specialized in game development, animation, and multimedia systems to merge creativity with technology.

Bachelor of Science in Data Science & Analytics

Equips students with data analysis, statistical computing, and machine learning skills to handle real-world big data challenges.

Engineering Programs

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE)

Prepares students for careers in construction, infrastructure development, and structural design.

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME)

Develops knowledge in machine design, manufacturing processes, and thermodynamics for industry applications.

Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE)

Focuses on power generation, transmission, distribution, and electrical system design.

Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering (BSECE)

Equips students with skills in telecommunications, embedded systems, robotics, and circuit design.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE)

Integrates computer science and engineering principles, specializing in hardware, software, and embedded systems.

Other Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA)

Provides expertise in management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship for future business leaders.

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA)

Prepares students for professional practice in auditing, taxation, and financial management.

Bachelor of Science in Education (BSEd)

Trains future educators in pedagogy, curriculum development, and classroom management.

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

Develops professional nurses skilled in patient care, clinical practice, and healthcare leadership.

Bachelor of Laws (LLB) / Juris Doctor (JD)

Prepares students for the legal profession, focusing on constitutional, criminal, civil, and corporate law.

Bachelor of Arts in Psychology (AB Psych)

Explores human behavior and mental processes, equipping students for careers in counseling, HR, and research.
